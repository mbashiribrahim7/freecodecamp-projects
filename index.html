A berief history of Sir. Abubakar Tafawa Balewa

  • 1912 Born in Northern protoctorate Bauchi state nigeria.
  • 1933 Finishes his five year western education and returned to teach at Bauchi middle school.
  • 1941 became acquinted with the young Aminu Kano who was posted as the new teacher and same year promoted to headmaster.
  • 1944 He and some other teachers were selected from the north to study at the University of London's insititute of Education.
  • 1946 He was elected to the Northern House os Assembly.
  • 1947 He was elected to the Legistilative Council.
  • 1951 Ahmadu Bello and him formed a political party knowns as Jam'iyyar Mutanen Arewa in english as Northern People's Congress.
  • 1952 He served as the Minister of Works and then later as the Minister of Transportation when Nigeria was moving towards sel-government.
  • 1957 He became Chief Minister and designated Prime Minister after his party won election.
  • 1960 He was regained his post after Nigeria got independence.
  • 1960 He led protest against Sharpeville massacare.
  • 1960-1964 He was instrumental in negotiations between Moise Thsombe and congolese authority during Congo crisis.
  • 1961 He entered allience with Commonwealth ministers who wanted south Africa to leave commonwealth.
  • 1964 He was re-elected as the chief prime Minister.
  • 1966 He died in military coup of January.
